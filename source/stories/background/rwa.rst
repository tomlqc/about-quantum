
Rotating Wave Approximation
===========================

.. include:: ../../qutex.rst

.. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------

:draft:`Draft Summary`

Change to the rotating reference frame,
equivalent to neglecting terms of higher frequency, that average out,
see :cite:`Haroche2013` p. 146 and p. 152 (Jaynes-Cumming).

.. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------

- | from p.165 :cite:`Loudon2000`:
  | "It is shown in Chapters 8 and 9 that the **non-energy-conserving terms** in :math:`\hatsubsup{H}{ED}{}`
    make important contributions to higher-order radiative processes, where energy is conserved in the
    final state but not in some of the intermediate states.
